Class Cheat Sheet

Artificer

Primary Attribute: Intelligence

Secondary Attribute: Constitution

Tertiary Attribute: Dexterity

Suggested Feats: Fey Touched, Tough, Warcaster

Suggested Spells

  • Cantrips: Fire Bolt, Guidance
  • 1st Level: Cure Wounds, Faerie Fire
  • 2nd Level: Invisibility, Web
  • 3rd Level: Haste, Fly
  • 4th Level: Freedom of Movement, Otiluke's Resilient Sphere
  • 5th Level: Animate Objects, Bigby's Hand

Barbarian

Primary Attribute: Strength

Secondary Attribute: Constitution

Tertiary Attribute: Dexterity

Suggested Feats: Great Weapon Master, Mobile, Sentinel, Tough

Bard

Primary Attribute: Charisma

Secondary Attributes: Dexterity

Tertiary Attribute: Constitution OR Intelligence

Suggested Feats: Actor, Fey Touched, Inspiring Leader, Resilient(CON)

Suggested Spells

  • Cantrips: Vicious Mockery, Minor Illusion
  • 1st Level: Faerie Fire, Healing Word, Silvery Barbs*
  • 2nd Level: Invisibility, Shatter
  • 3rd Level: Dispel Magic, Hypnotic Pattern
  • 4th Level: Dimension Door, Polymorph
  • 5th Level: Animate Objects, Teleportation Circle
  • 6th Level: Eyebite, Mass Suggestion
  • 7th Level: Forcecage, Teleport
  • 8th Level: Dominate Monster, Glibness
  • 9th Level: Foresight, True Polymorph

Suggested Magical Secrets Spells

  • Lore Bard Level 6 Secrets: Aura of Vitality, Counterspell, Fireball, Haste
  • Level 10 Secrets: Find Greater Steed, Circle of Power, Shadow of Moil
  • Level 14 Secrets: Contingency, Heal, Plane Shift
  • Level 18 Secrets: Illusory Dragon, Mass Heal, Wish

*Some DMs ban these spells, talk to your DM before taking it

Cleric

Primary Attribute: Wisdom

Secondary Attributes: Strength OR Dexterity

Tertiary Attribute: Constitution

Suggested Feats: Fey Touched, Resilient(CON), Warcaster

Suggested Spells

  • Cantrips: Guidance, Light, Sacred Flame, Toll the Dead
  • 1st Level: Cure Wounds, Guiding Bolt, Healing Word
  • 2nd Level: Hold Person, Prayer of Healing, Spiritual Weapon
  • 3rd Level: Aura of Vitality*, Dispel Magic, Revivify, Spirit Guardians
  • 4th Level: Banishment, Death Ward
  • 5th Level: Holy Weapon, Mass Cure Wounds
  • 6th Level: Heal, Heroes' Feast
  • 7th Level: Conjure Celestial, Regenerate
  • 8th Level: Antimagic Field, Holy Aura
  • 9th Level: Mass Heal

*Some DMs ban these spells, talk to your DM before taking it
